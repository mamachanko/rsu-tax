Your Schwab Files

You'll download up to 3 files from Schwab. Here's where to find each one.

Realized Gain/Loss (Required)

Your main transaction data — every lot you sold.

  • Log in to Equity Award Center
  • Click Realized Gain/Loss in the left sidebar
  • Set the date range to cover your tax year
  • Click Export (top right) → CSV

Lapse History (Recommended)

Gives us vest dates and FMV per share — so we convert cost basis at the correct EUR rate.

  • In Equity Award Center, go to your transaction history
  • Filter by transaction type: Lapse
  • Set date range to cover your tax year
  • Click Export → CSV

1042-S Tax Form (Optional)

If you want to claim a foreign tax credit on your German return (Anlage AUS).

  • On schwab.com, go to Accounts → Statements
  • Click Tax Forms tab
  • Find your 1042-S for the relevant tax year
  • Download PDF
After these steps you should have:
✓ realized-gain-loss.csv REQUIRED
✓ lapse-history.csv RECOMMENDED
○ 1042-S.pdf OPTIONAL
Note: You cannot use the USD gain/loss figure from Schwab directly for German taxes. German tax law requires converting proceeds and cost basis to EUR separately at the ECB rate for each respective date.
